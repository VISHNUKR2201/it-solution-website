# Product Overview

## Stackly - IT Solutions Website

Stackly is a comprehensive IT solutions company website that provides digital services and technology consulting. The website showcases the company's services, projects, team, and career opportunities.

### Key Features
- Multi-page corporate website with modern design
- Responsive layout for all devices
- Service portfolio including web development, cloud solutions, and cybersecurity
- Project showcase and case studies
- Team member profiles and company information
- Career opportunities and HR employer sections
- Contact forms and business information
- User authentication system (login/dashboard)

### Target Audience
- Businesses seeking IT solutions and consulting
- Potential clients looking for web development and cloud services
- Job seekers interested in IT career opportunities
- HR professionals and employers

### Business Sections
- **Services**: Web development, cloud architecture, cybersecurity, GIS solutions
- **Projects**: Case studies, client testimonials, recent work showcase
- **About**: Company information, team profiles, values and culture
- **Career**: Job opportunities, company culture, application process
- **HR Employers**: Recruitment services and employer resources
